Who We Are & Our Vision

About Youth Spark

A transformative movement growing value-driven leaders across Kenya and Africa. We focus on shaping the 7 mountains of influence, culture, and ministry.


  • Vision: Discipling Nations through the Gospel of the Kingdom.
  • Mission: To disciple and equip young leaders through Kingdom-centered leadership training, practical skills development, and industry-focused mentorship — empowering them to transform industries, shape culture, influence societies, and advance nation-building with excellence.
